The ultimate recipe that completely changed the chicken industry is... pickle juice?

YUP!! The crazy success from Chick-fil-a is led by maybe one of the weirdest ingredients ever.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken strips
  • 1 cup dill pickle juice
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 Tbsp salt and pepper
  • 1/2 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp onion powder
  • Peanut oil

How to Make Copycat Chick-fil-a Strips

  1. Place chicken tenders in ziploc bag, add pickle juice and refrigerate at least 4 hours
  2. Remove from the bag and pat dry with a paper towel
  3. Add egg and milk in small bowl and beat
  4. Add flour, paprika, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder to another bowl
  5. Add peanut oil to large skillet, Heat to medium
  6. Dredge chicken in egg mixture then flour mixture
  7. Add chicken to oil and cook 4-5 minutes until golden brown
  8. Drain on paper towels, and serve warm
  9. Enjoy!

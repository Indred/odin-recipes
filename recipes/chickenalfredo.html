Chicken Alfredo

The dish that makes life delish!

While Chicken Alfredo isn't traditional italian, it's still more protein-based and scrumptious!

Chicken Alfredo is one of the most popular dishes on the planet, you'll love it.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz dry fettucine pasta
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3/4 Tbsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 Tbsp pepper
  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • Alfredo sauce

How to Make Chicken Alfredo

  1. Boil the fettucine pasta in salted water pot
  2. Season chicken breast with salt and pepper, and pan fry
  3. Transfer the chicken onto a cutting board cut into 1/2-inch-thick slices
  4. Add the chicken and alfredo sauce into the noodle pot
  5. Mix the alfredo and chicken
  6. Garnish with parsley, and serve warm
  7. Enjoy!

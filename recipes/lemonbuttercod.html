Lemon Butter Cod

A sweet and savoury dish!

This recipe is my favorite way to prepare cod!

Cod is generally okay, but this recipe changes that with an addition of 3 ingredients: lemon, butter, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Cod fillets (Best if 1-inch thick)
  • Flour, soaks up moisture
  • Seasonings: paprika, onion powder, thyme, oregano, cayenne pepper, salt and pepper
  • Butter, fresh stick, preferably unsalted
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Parsley, for the finishing touch

How to Make Lemon Butter Cod

  1. Mix flour and seasonings
  2. Spread mixture over cod
  3. Heat 1 Tbsp butter in skillet
  4. Add and sear cod until golden brown
  5. Melt the remaining 4 Tbsp of butter
  6. Add and saute garlic until color change
  7. Sprinkle with parsley and serve warm
  8. Enjoy!
